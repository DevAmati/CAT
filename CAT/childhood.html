Childhood Memories

Hi there! I'm Morgan, and this is a glimpse into my wonderful childhood.

I spent my early years living with my grandma, who filled my days with love and warmth. It was a time of joy and innocence, surrounded by the care of a wise and loving grandmother.

After school, I had three amazing friends who made every afternoon an adventure. We played together, laughed together, and created memories that I'll cherish forever.

My educational journey began at Masaba Primary School, where I took my first steps into the world of learning. The school became a place of discovery, friendship, and growth.

Come join me on this nostalgic journey as I recount the tales of a time when life was simple, and every moment was filled with wonder!
